My Favourite Travel Destination: SKARDU VALLEY

Skardu Valley, nestled in Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan, is a breathtaking destination. Surrounded by the majestic Karakoram mountains, it offers stunning landscapes and serene lakes. Deosai National Park, Satpara Lake, and Kachura Lakes are must-visit attractions. Rich in Balti culture and history, Skardu Valley is a treasure trove of ancient forts and monasteries. A paradise for trekkers, climbers, and nature lovers, Skardu Valley is a unique experience.

TOP ATTRACTIONS

Natural Wonders

  1. Deosai National Park -A high-altitude plateau and home to the Himalayan brown bear, Deosai National Park offers stunning views, trekking trails, and wildlife spotting opportunities.
  2. Satpara Lake -A picturesque lake surrounded by majestic mountains, Satpara Lake is perfect for boating, fishing, and picnicking.
  3. Kachura Lakes -Two stunning lakes, Upper Kachura and Lower Kachura, offer breathtaking views, trekking trails, and picnic spots.
  4. Shangri-La Lake -A serene and picturesque lake surrounded by lush greenery and majestic mountains.

Historical and Cultural Attractions

  1. Skardu Fort -A 16th-century fort built by the rulers of Baltistan, Skardu Fort offers stunning views of the valley and the Indus River.
  2. Kharpocho Fort - A 16th-century fort built by the rulers of Baltistan, Kharpocho Fort offers stunning views of the valley and the Indus River.
  3. Manthal Buddha Rock -A 7th-century Buddha rock carving, Manthal Buddha Rock is a significant cultural and historical attraction.
  4. Shigar Valley - A picturesque valley surrounded by majestic mountains, Shigar Valley offers stunning views, trekking trails, and cultural experiences.

Top Activities To Enjoy

  • Trekking -Explore the valley's stunning landscapes and trek to nearby villages and lakes.
  • Hiking -Hike to the nearby mountains and enjoy breathtaking views of the valley.
  • Camping -Set up camp near the lakes or in the mountains and enjoy the serene beauty of nature.
  • Rafting -Experience the thrill of rafting on the Indus River.
  • Fishing -Try your hand at fishing in the lakes and rivers of Skardu Valley.
  • Boating -Enjoy a relaxing boat ride on Satpara Lake or other lakes in the valley.
  • Photography -Capture the breathtaking beauty of Skardu Valley through your lens.
  • Mountain Biking -Explore the valley's rugged terrain on a mountain bike.
CATEGORY DETAILS
Best Time To Visit Summer (June to August) Ideal for trekking, hiking, and camping. The weather is warm and sunny, with average temperatures ranging from 18°C to 25°C (64°F to 77°F).
Weather Summer (June to August) is warm and sunny with temperatures ranging from 18°C to 25°C, while Winter (December to February) is cold and snowy with temperatures ranging from -10°C to -20°C.
Average Cost Per Person The average cost for a person visiting Skardu Valley can range from PKR 50,000 to PKR 150,000 (approximately USD 300 to USD 900) per person for a 5-7 day trip.
Main Attraction Deosai National Park is the main attraction in Skardu, known for its breathtaking landscapes and home to the Himalayan brown bear.
